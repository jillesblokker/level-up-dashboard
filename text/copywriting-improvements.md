# Copywriting Improvements & Consistency Check

This document outlines suggested improvements to game text to enhance storytelling, consistency, and character personality.

## **1. Achievement Messages (Destruction & Discovery)**

The goal is to give each elemental creature a distinct personality:
*   **Fire (Flamio, Embera, Vulcana):** Hotheaded, energetic, impatient, destructive.
*   **Water (Dolpio, Divero, Flippur):** Playful, cool, relaxed, flowy.
*   **Earth (Rockie, Buldour, Montano):** Stoic, solid, grumpy about being moved.
*   **Ice (Icey, Hailey, Blizzey):** Cold, sharp, precise, slightly haughty.
*   **Nature (Leaf, Oaky, Seqoio):** Wise, ancient, slow-speaking, nurturing.

## **2. Tarot Card Descriptions**

The goal is to make these feel more like mystical prophecies or ancient wisdom, rather than generic flavor text.

| Card | Old Text | New Suggested Text |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Warrior** | "Strength flows through your veins." | "The steel sings to you. Your arm strikes with the weight of legends." |
| **The Scholar** | "Knowledge is power." | "The ink of history is not yet dry. Your mind is the quill that writes the future." |
